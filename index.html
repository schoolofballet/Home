Misty's School of Ballet

Misty's School of Ballet is a dance academy for all young aspiring artists interested in Ballet and other contemporary forms of dance. We follow a vetted development syllabus from The Royal Academy of Dance and it is going to be helpful in elevating our dancers. Thank you.

Let's get social!

  • Lessons from Pre-primary to Advanced level
  • Students from age 4 and upwards
  • Male and Female
  • RAD Ballet syllabus
  • Examinations and Eistedfords

Our studios are based in the Roodepoort Theatre 100 Christian De Wet Rd in florida Park.

Operating hours are all Saturdays @ 9AM.
During weekdays all classes start @ 3PM
